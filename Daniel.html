Daniel Radcliffe


ABOUT
Actor best known for playing the wizard-in-training Harry Potter in the landmark Harry Potter film franchise. The role earned him numerous awards including multiple Teen Choice Awards and MTV Movie Awards as well as a National Movie Award for Best Male Performance in 2007.

TRIVIA
After the final installment of Harry Potter, he played leading roles in the films The Woman in Black (2012), Kill Your Darlings (2013) and Horns (2014). He also earned recognition for his performance in the stage production How to Succeed in Business Without Really Trying.

FAMILY LIFE
His parents are literary agent Alan George Radcliffe and casting agent Marcia Jeannine Gresham. He dated actress Laura O'Toole, whom he met while acting in the stage production Equus, from 2007 to 2008. He began dating actress Erin Darke in 2012.

MOVIES
Harry Potter(Series), Swiss Army Man, Kill Your Darlings, The Woman In Black, Horns, Now You See Me 2, Imperium, Viktor Frankenstein, December Boys, Jungle, The Tailor Of Panama, Trainwreck, My Boy Jack, Beast Of Burden.
