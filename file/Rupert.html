Rupert Grint


ABOUT
Actor who won a Young Artist Award for Most Promising Young Newcomer for his role as Ron Weasley in Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone. He also starred in the films Driving Lessons and Cherrybomb.

TRIVIA
In 2011, he starred in the music video for Ed Sheeran's song "Lego House."

FAMILY LIFE
His mother was a housewife and his father sold memorabilia. He began dating actress Georgia Groome in 2011.

MOVIES
Harry Potter(Series), Driving Lessons, Moonwalkers, Cherrybomb, Wild Target, Charlie Countryman, Into The White, Thunderpants, Wartime Wanderers, Underdogs, CBGB.
